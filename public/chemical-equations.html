Chemical Equation Balancer

How to Play: Your goal is to make sure the number of atoms for each element is the same on both sides of the equation. This follows the Law of Conservation of Mass, which states that in a chemical reaction, matter is not created or destroyed.

Use the boxes to change the coefficients. The "Atom Counter" will update in real-time. When the count for an element is equal on both sides, it will turn green. Balance all the elements to solve the puzzle!
