Electron Orbital Filler

Rules for Filling Orbitals

  • Aufbau Principle: Electrons fill the lowest energy orbitals first. (1s → 2s → 2p → 3s → 3p → 4s)
  • Hund's Rule: Every orbital in a subshell is singly occupied with one electron before any one orbital is doubly occupied.
  • Pauli Exclusion Principle: All singly occupied orbitals have electrons with the same spin. When an orbital is doubly occupied, the electrons must have opposite spins (↑↓).
