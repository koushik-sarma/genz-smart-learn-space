Molecule Builder

How to Play: Drag atoms from the palette into the reaction area to see how they form chemical bonds. The simulation will automatically detect a valid combination and show you what happens!

Try to build these compounds:

  • Ionic Bonds (transfer electrons): NaCl, Na₂O, NaOH
  • Covalent Bonds (share electrons): H₂, O₂, HCl, H₂O, Cl₂O
