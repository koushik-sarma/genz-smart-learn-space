3D Molecule Shape Viewer

Hybridization is the idea that atomic orbitals mix to form new, identical hybrid orbitals. This process determines the 3D shape of a molecule.

  1. Click a button to select a molecule.
  2. Watch the atomic 's' and 'p' orbitals animate and "morph" into hybrid orbitals.
  3. Finally, the 3D molecule will appear. Click and drag with your mouse to rotate it!
