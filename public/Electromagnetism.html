Chapter 10: Electromagnetism Visualizer

Part 1: Magnetic Field from a Current

Oersted's Experiment

A current flowing through a wire creates a circular magnetic field around it. The direction of the field follows the Right-Hand Grip Rule. Drag the compass to see how the field changes with position.

Part 2: The Motor Principle (Lorentz Force)

Force on a Wire (F = ILB)

When a current-carrying wire is placed in a magnetic field, it experiences a force. The direction of this force is given by the Right-Hand Rule. Increase the current to see the force increase and move the wire.
