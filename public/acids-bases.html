Virtual pH Lab

What is pH? The pH scale measures how acidic or basic a substance is. It ranges from 0 to 14. A pH of 7 is neutral (like pure water). A pH less than 7 is acidic, and a pH greater than 7 is basic (or alkaline).

How to use this lab:

  1. Drag an indicator (like Litmus) onto a test tube to see the color change.
  2. The digital meter will show the substance's pH and information about it.
  3. Try the Neutralization simulator to see what happens when you mix an acid and a base!
